Week Two: Mighty Morphin' JavaScript

...but this time, using GitHub!!

or... 'A Procrastinator's Lament'

Ah, the "wisdom" of age. ???!@#$!? As Charlie was describing to us the mere 3-point deduction after about 3am, pleading with us to just 'Go to bed!', I smugly sat there thinking to myself, 'At least I'm too clever for THAT to happen to me!

Yet, the time management skills which I had thought I had mastered over the last few years have instead come crashing down around me. My problem, in programming, has ALWAYS been that I am a very [in fact entirely TOO] optimistic estimator of my own speed. I am consistently TOO optimistic in my projections of time needed. I just don't allow myself enough time, and it takes much, much longer than I think it 'should.'

And, come to find out, that optimism has come roaring back to haunt me here.

Yet at whom, save me, may I gnash my teeth in vigorous melodramatic frustration??

I can only hope that this time, at least, I will ACTUALLY learn to budget far, far more time than I expect to need.

To code... perchance to nap....

There are, it seems, several items I could keep fresh at the front of my mind for reference :

  • I am far too unfamiliar with the Canvas and MyBCC resources. They are just too new to me.
  • I have got to factor in some sort of 'curiosity' multiplier, as I seem to get distracted by tangents instead of focusing on the final goal. e.g.: 'ooo what precise colour can I find to please my eyes?'... then twenty-three samples later I realize... erm...maybe I just need to CODE.
  • As familiar as I really am with programming, I still need to allow time to get familiar with these new tools. Example: let Eclipse crash seven times before I finally STOP clicking on the Help - Change Font topic.

And now for something completely different....

My fiancee' still absolutely refuses to let me (us) get a bunny. I keep searching the seas of the Internet, hoping to find a picture that is just too darn cute for her to resist... but still a no go so far.
